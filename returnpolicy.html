Return Policy

1. Unopened Packages:
  • If you receive a product and it remains unopened, you are eligible for a full refund. Please ensure that the product is returned in its original, unused condition and packaging.
2. Opened Packages:
  • If you open the package but wish to return the product, a deduction of ₹100 will be applied to cover restocking and handling fees.
  • Returns must be initiated within 3 days of receiving the product. Any return requests made after 3 days will not be accepted.
3. Return Process:
  • Contact our customer service team to initiate a return and obtain a return authorization.
  • Package the product securely and ensure it is in its original condition.
  • Ship the product back to us as per the instructions provided.
4. Refund Processing:
  • Refunds for unopened packages will be processed in full upon receipt of the returned product.
  • Refunds for opened packages, after deducting ₹100, will be processed once the returned product is received and inspected.
5. Non-Returnable Items:
  • Products that have been used or damaged, or returned outside the 3-day window, are not eligible for a refund.
